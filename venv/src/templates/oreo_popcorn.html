Cookies & Cream Popcorn

Want a sweet snack while hanging out with friends? Cookies & Cream Popcorn is the way to go! Taking 10 minutes to prepare, this snack will put some pop into friendly gatherings. Oreos may be milk's favorite cookie, but with this snack, Oreos can be popcorn's perfect pal. You will need...

  • 24 oz. White Vanilla Almond Bark OR White Chocolate
  • 9 cups of popped popcorn
  • 20 Oreos, finely crushed

Step 1

Melt white vanilla almond bark or white chocolate according to package directions until completely melted. Warm up in microwave in 30-second increments until fully melted.

Step 2

Finely crush the 20 Oreos in a plastic bag. Make sure to squeeze out as much air as possible from the bag before beginning to crush. Multiple bags can be used if all Oreos don't fit in one bag.

Step 3

Pop the popcorn in a microwave and pour into a bowl. Sprinkle Oreo crumbs throughout popcorn to evenly distribute. Stir with spoon for extra mixing of the popcorn and Oreo crumbs.

Step 4

Use spoon to drizzle melted chocolate throughout the popcorn and Oreo mix. Mix to distribute the melted chocolate evenly. Allow chocolate to settle for one minute and dig in!
